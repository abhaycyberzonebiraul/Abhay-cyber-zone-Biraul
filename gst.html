GST Registration Kya Hai?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) ek indirect tax hai jo pure India me businesses ke liye zaroori hai. Ye aapke business ko ek proper legal identity deta hai aur growth me madad karta hai.

  • Business ko official legal identity milti hai
  • B2B invoice easily generate kar sakte hain
  • Amazon, Flipkart par online selling ke liye zaroori
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) ka fayda milta hai
